- Cinematic comic style, painterly digital illustration.
- Character consistency: IQ chicken (white body, expressive face, pink exposed brain crest, red comb feathers).
- Setting consistency: Russian storybook + retro-futuristic lab vibe.
- Rich lighting, high detail, warm-cool color contrast.
- No text bubbles, no watermarks.
- Aspect ratio close to 3:4 or 4:5.

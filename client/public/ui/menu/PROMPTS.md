# Menu/Scenario Art Prompts

Use this style guide for all menu and scenario states.

## Global Style
- Dark wooden fantasy laboratory mood.
- Golden frames and parchment accents.
- Comic storybook atmosphere with warm light.
- Keep text zones readable and uncluttered.
- Do not mix with a second visual reference style.

## Story Menu States
- `default`: neutral readable menu.
- `active/<button-id>`: selected button highlighted with warm golden glow.
- `locked/<button-id>`: muted dark overlay and reduced contrast.
- `frame-2`: same composition, slightly shifted atmosphere for frame toggle.

## Scenario States (`ui/scenarios/<screen>/`)
- `default`: normal functional state.
- `empty`: no data yet / waiting state.
- `success`: completion state with brighter glow.

## Per-Screen Emphasis
- `laboratory`: blue scientific glow.
- `barn`: warm amber farm light.
- `incubator`: golden egg aura.
- `collection`: shelf and trophy focus.
- `arena`: energetic combat ambiance.
- `settings`: control room calm tone.
